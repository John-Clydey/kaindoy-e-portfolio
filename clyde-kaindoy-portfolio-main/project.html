Scotch Yoke Mechanism Fabrication and Design

A simple mechanism that converts rotary motion into linear motion was initially designed in SolidWorks and subsequently fabricated using acrylic glass. This project showcases the seamless transition from digital design to physical realization, highlighting both precision in CAD modeling and the practical application of materials.
